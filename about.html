Juta Jaya uses state of the art technologies. Our unique arrangment of printing presses enable us to manage our client's printing need, from business cards, to paper bags and custom made souvenirs using full color marketing materials in the most efficient way.

Our Workflow

  • Cliant - Sales Consultation

    Upon the first client and our salesperson meeting, our salesperson will take note on what are the client needs.

  • Design Proposal

    Our salesperson will then brief our inhouse designer on what the client needs.

  • Clients' quality check

    Our designers will then show their various range of designs for the client to choose from.

  • Clients' approval

    Once the client has made this decision and the price has been agreed, our team then proceed to the pre-press printing.

  • Layout / CTP

    Layout will be set up and then proceeded to computer-to-plate machine.

  • Production / Finishing

    Once the CTP produced the required result, the utput will be transferred to our production team for mass production and go to the process of lamination or stitching, spot uv, die cutting etc.
